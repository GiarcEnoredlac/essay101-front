Write your way to success.

Welcome to Essay 101 - Leaders in high-stakes essay coaching and evaluation services.

We developed our passion for helping students achieve their educational goals in 2000 when students from Georgetown, Duke and Harvard formed a volunteer organization to critique and edit admissions essays of underprivileged high school students in Washington, DC and Miami. Students were thrilled with the results, and as news spread we were flooded with requests for editing services. Enter Essay101.

We are not just another editing company. We go beyond technical edits and revisions. We set our students up for success, ensuring that they have the confidence, knowledge, and guidance to achieve excellence throughout the admissions process and beyond.

Leading admissions and test prep companies rely on us to deliver personalized, actionable feedback to their students through our SAT/ACT essay and admissions essay evaluation services. Since our founding we have helped over 100,000 students achieve their admissions goals, find their voice, and strengthen their writing skills.

Our Editors

We work with only the best and brightest editors and have developed a rigorous screening and selection process to identify candidates who have what it takes to become an Essay101 editor.

Our editors are obsessed with words and language. They are grammar sticklers who know the most obscure rules. They find typos everywhere - from professional publications to restaurant menus - but aren’t know-it-alls. They get deep satisfaction from analyzing an essay and identifying the most impactful feedback ranging from high-level considerations, such as organization, logic and argument, to technical issues, such as grammar, syntax, and spelling. Driven by the desire to help students write their best essays and achieve their goals, they view the editing process as a learning experience for students, not just a technical exercise.
